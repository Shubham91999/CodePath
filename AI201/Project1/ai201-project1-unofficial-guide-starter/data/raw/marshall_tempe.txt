Marshall Tempe Location
Located at 1057 E Apache Rd, Marshall Tempe sits just an 11-minute walk from ASU’s campus. Its prime spot places residents close to the Sun Devil Fitness Complex and SDFC Intramural Fields, with plenty of restaurants, coffee shops, and nightlife just a short stroll away along Mill Avenue.
At Marshall, you’re just a short walk from the Barrett Dining Center and local favorites like Honey 2 Bee Coffee & Crepes and Ziggie’s Coffee. You’ll also be surrounded by other popular student housing communities such as

Amenities at Marshall Tempe
Rendering of pool deck at Marshall Tempe. Photo courtesy of Marshall Tempe.
Marshall Tempe delivers a modern student living experience built around balance, offering amenities that support both academic focus and personal well-being. Residents can stay active in the 24/7 fitness center with a sauna and yoga room or relax by the rooftop pool and hot tub while taking in scenic views of Tempe.
For study time, Marshall provides five private lounges and open air study areas designed to help you reset and refocus. When it’s time to unwind, you’ll find plenty of ways to connect, from the clubhouse and grilling stations to the fire pit and turf sports lawn. There’s even a dog spa on site, so your four-legged friends can enjoy the perks of student living too.
Parking at Marshall Tempe
If you plan on bringing a car to campus, Marshall Tempe offers convenient on-site parking for an additional monthly cost of $125 (as of publication). It’s best to check with the leasing team for the latest rates and availability.

Marshall Tempe Pricing and Floorplans
Representative 4×4 floor plan at Marshall Tempe. Photo courtesy of Marshall Tempe.
Marshall Tempe offers fully furnished apartments in a variety of layouts, including studio, one, two, three, and four bedroom options, making it easy to find a space that fits your lifestyle and budget.
The community is pet friendly, but additional costs related to bringing a pet are not currently listed on the Marshall’s website.
As of February 2026, rates for the 2026-2027 leasing period range from approximately $1,390 to $2,000 per person, per month, depending on the floor plan and unit type. Keep in mind that prices may change throughout the leasing season, as student housing communities often use a tiered system where rates increase as leases are signed.
