About Tempe Arizona
If you are planning to attend Arizona State University, finding student apartments in Tempe becomes necessary. Located in Maricopa County, Arizona, Tempe is home to over 200,000 residents. Among these, over 74,000 are students.
Tempe has a dry and hot desert climate with almost 4 months of summer. Even so, this college town is popular among students from across the world due to one of the largest universities in the US. Named after the Vale of Tempe of ancient Greece, the city was originally an agricultural town until World War II. Now, Tempe is the seventh-largest city in Arizona.
Home to Arizona State University (ASU), the city blends education, sports, and culture. With amber, you can choose Tempe student apartments close to the university to enjoy all that the city has to offer.

Room Types Available in Tempe
You can select from a range of room types while choosing for student apartments in Tempe. From studios for better privacy to shared rooms for never being alone, Tempe student rooms come in a variety of layouts. Here are the most popular ones.
1. Bedroom Apartments:
When you want a room all to yourself, but also need a small group of friends waiting on you when you get home late, choose bedroom apartments. Choose from 2-, 3-, 4-, or 5-bedroom layouts to share with a small or large group.
2. Studios:
If you’re someone whose social battery drains as the sun sets, studios might be a good option for you. These are individual units with a private bedroom, private kitchen, and private bathroom, all to yourself.
3. 1-Bedroom Apartments:
Want to live alone but also want to call friends over for a sleepover? 1-Bedroom apartments are perfect. These give you a private bedroom, a living room, a private kitchen, and a private bath.
4. Private Room:
A private room type can be available as a studio or in a shared apartment, too. With a private room, you may have an ensuite or a non-ensuite.
5. Shared Room:
Shared rooms are the least private of all the other options. These can be part of a studio or a shared-bedroom apartment. Shared rooms reduce rent and let you live comfortably while paying significantly less for the same room.

Student Travel In Tempe
Tempe offers a wide range of transportation options for students to choose from to travel across the city. These options vary from free to minimal fare with a monthly pass. Here’s all you need to know about student travel options in Tempe.
1. Valley Metro Bus:
The buses run on all the major bus routes across the city. A regular bus ride will cost you at least $2, or you can get a monthly pass for $64.
2. Tempe ORBIT:
This is a free shuttle that runs across 6 routes between Downtown Tempe and the ASU campus.
3. Light Rail:
METRO Light Rail is a great option for students who want to travel to Central Phoenix or Mesa quickly. The light rail also connects downtown to Sky Harbor Airport for students planning their journey further.
4. Bike:
As a cycling-friendly city, Tempe encourages students to ride bikes with 220 miles of bike lanes throughout the city.
