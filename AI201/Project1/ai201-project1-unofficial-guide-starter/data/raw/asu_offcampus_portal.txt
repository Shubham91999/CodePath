Why Students Choose Off-Campus Housing Near ASU
Thousands of Arizona State University students live off campus each year. The ASU Off-Campus Housing Marketplace is the official housing resource for students to help simplify their search for apartments, houses, roommate connections, and sublease opportunities near campus. Browse available housing listings, connect directly with landlords and property managers, find compatible roommates, and access leasing resources designed to simplify your housing search. Whether you're looking for your first apartment or your next place to live, you've come to the right place to find off-campus housing throughout Tempe and the greater Phoenix area.
