Pricing and Floorplans
Photo courtesy of olivtempe.
ōLiv Tempe offers fully-furnished apartments with flexible pricing options to suit different budgets. Floorplans range from studios to four-bedroom layouts. This community is also pet-friendly; if you plan to bring a pet, expect a one-time pet deposit of $200 and a recurring monthly pet fee of $35.
As of January 2026, rates for the 2025–2026 leasing period range from $1,150 to $1,980 per person, per month, depending on the unit type. Rates may vary throughout the lease-up season, as student housing communities typically use a tiered pricing system, with rates increasing as leases are signed. This means signing early can help you lock in lower rates before they increase closer to the school year.
