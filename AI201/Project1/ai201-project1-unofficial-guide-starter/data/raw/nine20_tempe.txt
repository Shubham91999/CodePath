Location
Located just a five-minute walk from ASU’s campus at 920 S Terrace Rd, Nine20 Tempe is ideally situated for students. The property offers easy access to key spots like Hayden Library, the Memorial Union and Mill Avenue’s vibrant dining and entertainment options.
Its proximity to campus facilities like the Sun Devil Marketplace and ASU’s fitness centers makes it convenient for both academic and recreational needs. If staying close to the energy of downtown Tempe and ASU is a priority, Nine20’s location is hard to beat.
Amenities
When comparing amenities, consider whether features like a 24/7 fitness center, study lounges, or social spaces are essential to your lifestyle and academic needs.
Nine20 Tempe boasts modern features designed with students in mind:
Pool & Sundeck: Enjoy the resort-style pool with an oversized hot tub.
24/7 Fitness Center: Includes yoga and boxing rooms for wellness-focused residents.
Study Spaces: Eight individual study areas and a business center with free printing.
Nine20 is a pet-friendly community that blends work and play, offering a lively living experience for ASU students. If these amenities fit your vibe, Nine20 could be a great option.
Parking
If you’re planning to bring a car to school, Nine20 Tempe has on-site parking for between $100 and $195 per month. While parking is available for residents, spaces aren’t reserved, so they may vary in availability.
Pricing and Floorplans
Nine20 Tempe offers fully furnished apartments with flexible pricing to fit a range of budgets. Choose from a variety of floorplans, including studio, 1×1, 2×2, 3×3 and 4×4 layouts. The community is also pet-friendly, expect a one-time pet deposit and a monthly pet fee if you plan to bring your furry friend.
As of February 2025, rates for the 2025–2026 leasing period range from $1,025 to $1,999 per person, per month, depending on the unit type. Rates may vary throughout the lease-up season, as student housing communities typically use a tiered pricing system, with rates increasing as leases are signed. This means that early signers often secure lower rates, so acting sooner can save you money.
