About the Event

The STEM for Kids Fair is a fun, interactive day where children can explore science, technology, engineering, and math through hands-on experiments, coding games, robotics demos, and creative maker stations designed to spark curiosity. This exciting event is designed for children aged 6-14, along with their parents and teachers, creating an engaging learning environment for the whole family. The fair encourages learning in a playful, accessible way while making science and technology exciting for everyone in the community. Schools, community groups, and organizations that promote STEM education will find this event perfect for inspiring the next generation of innovators.
